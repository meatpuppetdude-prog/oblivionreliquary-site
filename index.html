The Reliquary

Oblivion Reliquary Publishing exists to preserve stories that challenge certainty, explore possibility, and examine the hidden structures beneath ordinary experience. From speculative fiction and literary exploration to philosophical inquiry and absurd comedy, the Reliquary collects works connected not by genre, but by curiosity.
