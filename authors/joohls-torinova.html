Author

Joohls Torinova

Joohls Torinova writes speculative fiction exploring perception, memory, identity, uncertainty, and the fragile structures people mistake for reality. His work ranges from philosophical science fiction to psychological horror and literary speculation, often examining the spaces where certainty collapses and deeper questions emerge. He is the creator of the Beneath Reality series and the author of Fatality of Bliss.
