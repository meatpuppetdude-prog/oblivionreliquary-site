Author

Ana Dorne Severin

Ana Dorne Severin writes intimate literary fiction concerned with memory, loss, love, grief, and the hidden emotional lives people carry through ordinary days. Her stories often explore fragile human connections, aging, identity, and the ways people endure change. She is the author of Dream a Little Dream and other forthcoming works of literary and psychological fiction.
